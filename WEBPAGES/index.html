About AUTOPARK

AUTOPARK is a state-of-the-art smart parking system designed to streamline the parking process. Our system uses RFID tags at the entrance to automatically identify and log vehicles, ensuring a seamless entry and exit experience. In addition, AUTOPARK provides real-time parking slot availability, allowing users to easily find available parking spaces and reducing the time spent searching for a spot. Join us and experience the future of parking today!
